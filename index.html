Located on the 1st floor of Fairmont Plaza, Olive Tree has been the heartbeat of Pasadena's Mediterranean food scene. We take pride in being LGBTQ+ friendly and offering a space where everyone feels at home.

From our handmade pita to our signature 24-hour marinated shawarma, we believe that slow food is the best food. Whether you're here for a quick takeout or a long, leisurely dine-in experience, we're dedicated to your delight.
